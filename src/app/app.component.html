About About Somali Kids App

This is a colorful app with nice background music and Swwwwwwedish speech. The app offers seven decks of cards to browse through. Here are over 100 different words, concepts and phrases for the younger children. Among other things, you get to learn to count to 10, recognize letters and geometric shapes. The app is child-friendly but lacks rewards.
